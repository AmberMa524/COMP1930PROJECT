All that said, it looks fairly good, almost like new, and I’m starting to enjoy it and appreciate the low price relative to buying a new one. My wife had dropped our iPad Mini 1, destroying the screen. I replaced that screen, and 6 mths later, my daughter pushed too hard on the home button and cracked the LCD!.
Took a look around for a replacement LCD, and for $70 I could replace it myself. Did a little more research and found this iPad Mini 4 for a very reasonable price, and reviews were positive for the refurb.
Unit arrived within 2 days and so far I'm super impressed. Battery life is good. Screen has very minor hairline scratches that I cannot see when the screen is on, and the body also has a few very tiny dings on the edges. Overall it's in a 9/10 condition, for half what Apple sells their certified refurbs for. Pretty happy for the price!
